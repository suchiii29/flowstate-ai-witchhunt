# FlowState AI – WitchHunt Submission

[Live Demo](https://flowstate-ai-witchhunt.vercel.app/)

## Overview

FlowState AI is an AI-powered student productivity and study-planning platform built for the WitchHunt Education theme. It helps students plan their learning, stay consistent, and reflect on their progress through a simple and focused interface.

The project is designed to support students who need more structure, motivation, and personalized guidance in their daily study routine.

## Problem Statement

Many students struggle with:
- inconsistent study habits,
- poor time management,
- lack of personalized learning support,
- exam stress,
- and limited access to structured guidance.

FlowState AI addresses this by turning study goals into an actionable routine with AI-assisted planning and reflection.

## Solution

FlowState AI helps students:
- create personalized study plans,
- track their daily progress,
- reflect on what they studied,
- and receive practical feedback to improve consistency.

The experience is intentionally simple, fast, and student-friendly.

## Key Features

- **AI Study Planning**  
  Generates a personalized study plan based on the student’s inputs.

- **Daily Reflection**  
  Lets users record what they studied and how the day went.

- **Progress Tracking**  
  Helps students understand their consistency and study behavior.

- **Clean UI**  
  Designed to be distraction-free and easy to use on mobile and desktop.

- **Hackathon-Ready Workflow**  
  Built as a practical prototype for the WitchHunt Education theme.

## Why This Project

This project focuses on a real education problem: helping students study better with less confusion and more consistency. Instead of being a generic productivity tool, FlowState AI is built around learning support and habit building.

It aligns with the WitchHunt goal of creating AI solutions that improve learning outcomes and create measurable social impact. [web:97]
