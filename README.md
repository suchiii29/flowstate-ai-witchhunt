# FlowState AI – WitchHunt Submission

[Live Demo](https://flowstate-ai-witchhunt.vercel.app/this)

## Overview

FlowState AI is a student productivity and study-planning platform built for the WitchHunt Education theme. It helps students plan their study time, stay consistent, reflect on their progress, and build better habits with AI-powered support.

The goal is to make learning more accessible, personalized, and practical for students who need structure, motivation, and simple guidance in their daily routine.

## Problem Statement

Many students struggle with:
- poor time management,
- inconsistent study habits,
- lack of personalized guidance,
- pressure from exams and deadlines,
- and limited access to supportive learning tools.

FlowState AI addresses this by turning raw study goals into a guided, AI-assisted routine that helps students focus, reflect, and improve.

## Solution

FlowState AI provides:
- AI-generated study planning,
- reflection and feedback after study sessions,
- progress tracking,
- and a clean interface designed for fast, distraction-free use.

It is built to be useful for students preparing for exams, managing multiple subjects, or trying to build discipline in a busy environment.

## Key Features

- **AI Study Plan**  
  Generates a personalized weekly plan based on user input such as subjects, available time, and goals.

- **Daily Reflection**  
  Lets students record what they actually studied and receive supportive AI feedback.

- **Progress Tracking**  
  Shows consistency, task completion, and study behavior over time.

- **Student-Friendly UI**  
  Simple interface designed for easy use on laptop and mobile.

- **AI Integration**  
  Uses AI to make planning and reflection more personalized and actionable.

## Why This Project

This project was built for the Education theme of WitchHunt because it focuses on improving learning outcomes through practical AI. Instead of being a generic productivity app, it is designed to help students become more consistent, organized, and confident in their studies.
